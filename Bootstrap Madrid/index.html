Introduction

With a metropolitan area population of approximately 6.5 million, Madrid is the third-largest city in the European Union. As the capital city of Spain, seat of government, and residence of the Spanish monarch, Madrid is also the political, economic and cultural centre of Spain.

Discover the Madrid that you want

Whether you’re travelling on a budget or willing to treat yourself to luxury experiences, you’ll find Madrid a fascinating destination. Leisure and cultural options range from admission-free museums to exclusive VIP spots.
